This Week's Actions
1
Enable Pre-Orders on Backordered Quilt Covers
Five quilt cover SKUs are backordered and losing sales daily. Use your existing Notify! Back in Stock / PreOrder app to switch these from "Sold Out" to "Pre-Order - Ships in 2-3 weeks". Customers who searched "linen quilt cover" (412 searches this week) are ready to buy - don't make them wait for a back-in-stock email. Capture the sale now.
Est. Impact: +$4,000-$6,000/week recovered - Priority: URGENT
2
Audit Mobile Checkout - Focus on Afterpay + Address Autofill
Test the full checkout flow on iPhone and Android. Common friction points for Shopify Plus stores with Afterpay: the Afterpay widget takes 2-3 seconds to render on mobile, creating a perceived "broken" checkout. Also test Shop Pay, Apple Pay, and Google Pay toggles - these should be the first visible option on mobile to reduce form-filling.
Est. Impact: +$4,200-$5,600/week - Priority: HIGH
3
Shift Klaviyo Campaign Send to Saturday 5pm AEST
Sunday drives 21% of weekly revenue (55 orders, $6,650). Your email channel converts at 3.6% - the highest of any channel. Move your primary weekly campaign from its current weekday slot to Saturday 5pm AEST to land in inboxes during the evening browsing window that feeds into Sunday purchases.
Est. Impact: +$1,200-$1,800/week - Priority: HIGH
4
Redirect Google Ads Budget to Pinterest Promoted Pins
Google Ads returned 1.2x ROAS ($1,025 spend to $1,230 revenue). Pinterest returned 5.2x ($792 spend to $4,120 revenue). Your handmade, bohemian aesthetic is purpose-built for Pinterest. Shift $750/week from Google broad match to Pinterest, specifically targeting "bohemian bedroom", "linen bedding australia", and "boho homewares" pins.
Est. Impact: +$2,400/week at same spend - Priority: MEDIUM
5
Improve "Silk Pillowcase" Collection Page Conversion
Searchspring shows 204 searches for "silk pillowcase" but only 6.1% conversion - well below the site average of 14%. Visitors are searching for silk pillowcases but not buying. Check: is the Silk Edit collection page surfacing the right products? Consider adding comparison content (silk vs cotton vs linen), and feature Okendo reviews on the collection page, not just product pages.
Est. Impact: +$600-$900/week - Priority: MEDIUM
